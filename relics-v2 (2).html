📜 How to Play

🎯 WIN

Collect all 5 Sacred Relics OR complete your Secret Mission

🗺️ THE WORLD

3 islands: War (combat), Prophecy (Oracle power), Dead (relics). Unvisited temples are hidden fog.

❤️ HP

Each player has 10 HP. Losing battles drains HP. At 0 you lose a relic and respawn.

💰 COSTS

Claim: 3🪙 | Ambush: 2🪨 | Upgrade L2: 3🪙+1📜 | Upgrade L3: 5🪙+2💎 | Ability: 2🪨 or 3🪙 | Ancient: 3🪙

🗡️ AMBUSH

Set on your temple (2 stone). Next opponent who enters gets -2 to their battle roll.

👁️ ANCIENT ONE

Every 5 turns a god awakens and changes the entire board. Summon early for 3 gold.

🔗 COMBOS

Collect 2+ Oracle cards then use COMBO to merge them for devastating super effects.
